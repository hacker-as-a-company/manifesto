# Hacker as a Company — Charter

Freedom with structure. Ethics without compromise. Community over ego.

## Principles

- Freedom ≠ chaos: autonomy requires discipline and professionalism.
- Ethics > ego: legality, consent, and integrity come first.
- No illegal operations under this name.
- Respect clients, peers, privacy, and data.
- Contribution > competition: share knowledge, improve tools, credit others.
- Transparency builds trust: clear scope, communication, and deliverables.

## Participation

- Sign the manifesto by starring or forking the repo.
- Follow the Code of Conduct (`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`).
- Propose changes via GitHub pull requests.
- Use the templates in `templates/` for client work, adapted to your laws.

## Governance

- Founder as Gardener: El Mehdi Oumedlouz acts as origin and steward — not CEO.
- Decisions prioritize the manifesto and charter alignment.
- Local cells (e.g., “Hacker as a Company – Morocco”) may self-organize while honoring this charter and license.
