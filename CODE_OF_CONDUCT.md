# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and professional community.

## Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- No harassment, hate, or illegal activity.
- Assume good intent; critique ideas, not people.
- Protect privacy and sensitive information.

## Reporting

- Report violations via GitHub issues or privately to maintainers.
- Maintainers may take any action deemed appropriate, including removal of contributions.

## Scope

- Applies to all project spaces: GitHub, chat rooms, events, and external representations of the project.
