# Pricing Guide (Reference)

> Calibrate to your market, skill, and risk.

## Models

- Fixed-price per project with clear scope.
- Time & materials (daily/weekly rate) with capped budget.
- Retainer for ongoing advisory and rapid response.
- Outcome-based (e.g., per remediation milestone) - use carefully.

## Benchmarks (example ranges)

- Solo pentest (SMB web app): $3k–$10k
- Cloud review (small env): $5k–$15k
- Red team (2–4 weeks): $15k–$60k
- Advisory retainer: $1k–$5k/month

## Levers

- Scope complexity and depth
- Evidence and reporting quality
- Speed/availability and timezone
- Risk and insurance requirements
- Reputation and references

## Good Practices

- Publish a basic rate card to avoid misalignment.
- Offer tiers (Basic / Plus / Premium) with clear deliverables.
- Take deposits; define payment schedule.
- Avoid unpaid “test tasks”.
